# Local SEO in Kenya: What Actually Moves the Needle

Ranking in Kenya means winning on mobile, local intent, and trust signals — not just stuffing keywords.

## Technical foundations

- Fast hosting and optimized images (many users are on 4G).
- Valid structured data for local business and services.
- Clean URLs, sitemaps, and indexable pages.

## Google Business Profile

For service businesses, a complete GBP listing with photos, categories, and regular posts often outperforms blog content alone. Encourage genuine reviews and respond promptly.

## Content that converts

Write for problems your customers search: pricing, location, “near me”, and comparison queries. Pair blog content with clear contact CTAs.

## Measure what matters

Track calls, form submissions, and WhatsApp clicks — not just rankings. GA4 events tied to business goals tell you if SEO spend is working.
